Privacy Policy

Depthify primarily imports, manages, and presents images that you choose on your device, including 2D viewing, spatial scene viewing, favorites, export, and purchase-unlock features. In the current version, we do not upload your selected images or their metadata to developer-operated servers, except for Apple-provided system services involved in purchases and system pickers.

Information We Process

  • When you choose images from the Photos library or files, the app reads that image data and stores an imported copy inside the app sandbox on your device.
  • To present image details, the app may read metadata stored in the image locally, such as filename, file size, resolution, capture time, camera information, and embedded location information if the original image contains it.
  • When you purchase, subscribe, or restore purchases, the app uses Apple's StoreKit services to read purchase-related status information such as product identifiers, subscription status, and expiration dates in order to unlock paid features.
  • The app also stores a limited amount of local preference and state data on device, such as favorite markers, presentation preferences, and purchase-related reminder settings.

How We Use Information

  • To import, store, sort, favorite, delete, and export images that you selected.
  • To generate thumbnails, read metadata, and display images in windowed or spatial-scene viewing modes on device.
  • To determine your purchase or subscription status and decide whether paid features such as spatial viewing should be unlocked.

Storage and Retention

  • Imported images are stored locally in the app's Application Support directory on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • Favorite status and certain local preferences are stored on device so your state can be restored the next time you open the app.
  • The current version does not provide developer-operated cloud sync and does not upload imported images, EXIF data, or location metadata to developer servers.

Your Choices

  • You can stop providing new images at any time by not importing additional content.
  • You can delete imported images inside the app. Deleting an imported image removes the local copy managed by the app from your device.
  • You can revoke photo access through Apple's system privacy settings.
  • If you no longer want the app to retain locally stored images and preferences, you can delete imported content in the app or uninstall the app.
  • Because the current version does not maintain developer-operated user accounts or cloud storage for your image library, there is no separate server-side account deletion workflow for imported images.

Sharing with Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal information to advertisers and we do not share imported image content for advertising purposes.
  • Purchases, subscriptions, and restore operations rely on Apple StoreKit and App Store services, which may process transaction-related data under Apple's own privacy policies.
  • If you import content through the system photo picker or file importer, the corresponding permission and system interaction are handled by Apple's frameworks.

Permissions

  • Photo access is used only to import photos that you actively select.
  • File access is used only to read image files that you actively choose to import.
  • If an image includes EXIF or location metadata, the app may display that information locally; you decide whether to import or export those images.

Analytics and Advertising

Based on the current implementation, the app does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not include third-party analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs.

Updates and Contact

  • We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's features or data practices change, and we will update the effective date in a future release.
  • If you have privacy questions, please contact: sunliang.lsl@gmail.com.
简体中文

隐私政策

Depthify 主要在设备本地导入、管理和展示你选择的图片，包括 2D 预览、空间场景查看、收藏、导出和购买解锁功能。除 Apple 提供的系统服务外，当前版本不会将你选择的图片或其元数据上传到开发者自有服务器。

我们会处理哪些信息

  • 当你从照片库或文件中主动选择图片时，App 会读取这些图片数据，并将导入后的副本保存到设备本地的应用沙盒中。
  • 为了展示图片信息，App 可能会在本地读取图片包含的元数据，例如文件名、文件大小、分辨率、拍摄时间、相机信息以及图片中自带的位置信息。如果原图包含这些内容，App 可能会展示这些信息。
  • 当你购买、订阅或恢复购买时，App 会通过 Apple 的 StoreKit 读取与内购状态相关的信息，例如商品标识、订阅状态和到期时间，用于验证你是否已解锁付费功能。
  • App 还会在本地保存少量偏好设置和状态信息，例如收藏标记、界面展示偏好以及与购买提醒相关的本地设置。

我们如何使用这些信息

  • 用于导入、保存、排序、收藏、删除和导出你选择的图片。
  • 用于生成缩略图、读取元数据，并在窗口模式或空间场景模式中展示图片。
  • 用于识别你的购买或订阅状态，并决定是否解锁空间场景等付费功能。

数据存储与保留

  • 导入后的图片会保存在你设备本地的应用支持目录中，直到你主动删除它们或卸载 App。
  • 收藏状态和部分本地偏好会保存在设备本地，用于下次启动时恢复你的使用状态。
  • 当前版本不提供开发者自有云同步服务，也不会将你导入的图片、EXIF 信息或位置信息上传到开发者服务器。

你的选择与控制

  • 你可以随时停止继续导入新的图片内容。
  • 你可以在 App 内删除已导入图片；删除后，App 管理的本地副本会从设备中移除。
  • 你可以在 Apple 系统隐私设置中撤回照片访问权限。
  • 如果你不希望 App 继续保留本地图片和偏好设置，可以在 App 内删除相关内容，或直接卸载 App。
  • 由于当前版本没有开发者自有账号系统，也没有为图片库提供开发者自有云存储，因此不存在单独的服务器端账号删除流程。

与第三方的共享

  • 我们不会向广告商出售你的个人信息，也不会出于广告投放目的共享你导入的图片内容。
  • 购买、订阅和恢复购买依赖 Apple 提供的 StoreKit 与 App Store 服务；这些服务可能按照 Apple 自身的隐私政策处理相关交易数据。
  • 如果你通过系统照片选择器或文件选择器导入内容，相关权限和系统交互由 Apple 的系统框架管理。

权限说明

  • 照片访问仅用于导入你主动选择的照片。
  • 文件访问仅用于读取你主动选择导入的图片文件。
  • 如果图片本身包含 EXIF 或位置信息，App 可能在本地展示这些信息；是否导入和导出这些图片由你自行决定。

第三方分析与广告

根据当前版本的代码实现，App 未集成第三方广告 SDK，也未集成第三方统计或行为分析 SDK。

政策更新与联系

  • 如果 App 的功能或数据处理方式发生变化，我们可能更新本隐私政策，并在新版本中标注更新时间。
  • 如你对隐私相关问题有疑问，请联系：sunliang.lsl@gmail.com。
